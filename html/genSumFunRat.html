Excess Funds Raised Per Category

What is this?

This table reports the amount of total excess funds raised per category as a multiplier of total goal amount

ie: A value of 2.0 in column 'Excess Funds Raised Multiple' signifies twice as many dollars have been raised compared to the total goal amount for the entire category

Notable Observations

  • Engineering related categories like "Design", "Games", and "Technology" tend to raise the most amount of excess funds
  • Entertainment related categories like "Dance", "Theater", and "Music" tend to raise funds closer to their goal amount

Client Recommendation

  • Have realistic expectations if your campaign does not fall under a technology or engineering category
  • Strongly consider Kickstarter if you have a project that is focused on engineering or technology
