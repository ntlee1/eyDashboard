Percent of Goal Reached

What Is This?

This is a plot of how close failed campaigns came to reaching their goal amount as a percentage.

ie: A project in the percent bin `(0,10]` raised less than 10% of its required goal amount.

Notable Observations

  • The majority of campaigns on Kickstarter don't even manage to raise 10% of their goal amount
  • It is a rare occurrence for a campaign to come close to reaching its goal amount. Either it succeeds or fails badly.

Client Recommendation

  • If you have a launched a campaign on Kickstarter and have not seen major traction within two weeks, save time and launch again.
  • Kickstarter heavily favors a binary success system. If a campaign fails badly do not be discouraged.
