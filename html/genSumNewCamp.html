Kickstarter Campaigns Launched by Year

What Is This?

This is a plot containing the number of Kickstarter campaigns launched by year from May 2009 - Dec 2018

Notable Observations

  • The Kickstarter platform has drastically increased in popularity since its launch in 2009
  • The number of successful campaigns have remained relatively steady 2012 onward
  • Large and Premium sized campaigns account for most of the recent growth in failed campaigns
  • Small and Mid sized campaigns under $4,000 have a relatively higher chance of succeeding

Client Recommendation

  • Consider launching a project on Kickstarter if capital needs are less than $10,000
  • Be prepared for a lower chance of success if capital needs are in excess of $10,000
